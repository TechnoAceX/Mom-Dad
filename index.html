21 Years of Love

A Beautiful Journey

Celebrating two decades of an unbreakable bond, countless memories, and a love that grows stronger with each passing day.

2003

Love at First Sight

"It all started with a glance… and a heartbeat."

In a moment that would forever change their lives, their eyes met across the room. A connection so deep and immediate that time seemed to stand still. Like stars aligning in the vast universe, their souls recognized each other at that very first glance.

2004

Marriage

"On 29th April 2004, two hearts became one — promising forever."

They joined hands and hearts, promising to walk together through life's every joy and sorrow. Their vows weren't just words but sacred promises written in the stars. A commitment to love each other through every season of life.

2005

A Prince Is Born

"26 Nov'2005 Their love materialized into a precious gift — their prince."

In 2005, their love story took a beautiful new turn — I was born. A perfect blend of both my incredible parents, I arrived as their little prince, filling their world with a joy they never knew existed. With my first cry, everything changed — laughter became louder, love grew deeper, and their bond became unbreakable. I wasn't just their son… I became the living proof of their love, their journey, and the dreams they built together. That year, our story became a family story.

2004-2011

Dubai Years

"A Journey Through the Desert Winds"

After their wedding, they moved to Dubai — a city that promised dreams but brought its own share of struggles. Long hours, silent sacrifices, and restless nights marked their journey. Yet, through it all, they shielded me with love. I still remember terrace dinners under the stars and the thrill of watching airplanes overhead. In 2011, with strength and hope, we left that life behind and returned to India — ready to build the happiness they truly deserved.

2011

Returning to India

"Back to India… with dreams, night shifts, weekend work — and love that never faded."

Returning to their roots, they carried with them dreams bigger than before. Through long night shifts and endless weekend work, their love remained their guiding light. In each other's eyes, they found the strength to persevere through every challenge.

2012-2024

The Dream of a Home

"Every fight, every tear… was for one dream — a home filled with love."

For years, they shared a vision of a place to call their own. Through every disagreement and every challenge, that dream kept them focused. Their determination never wavered, knowing that someday they would build a sanctuary filled with love.

Beautiful Memories

"One of their most beautiful memories…"

In 2021, we created one of our most cherished memories — a full family getaway to the City of Lakes, Udaipur. Nestled in a luxurious hotel, surrounded by love, laughter, and stunning views, we lived like royalty for a while. It wasn’t just a trip; it was pure joy — together, relaxed, and truly happy. A perfect chapter in our family story.

The Weight of Dreams

"And the Struggle Continued…......"

Even after coming back to India, life didn’t get easier. The battle shifted — from survival to building a dream. Financial stress, endless sacrifices, and the exhausting wait for our own home tested them in every way. It was one of the toughest chapters — but through every sleepless night and silent prayer, they held onto one thing: hope. The hope that someday, our dream home would stand tall, built with love, tears, and unshakable faith...

2023

The Day Our Hearts Hopped with Joy

"2nd June 2023 🐰💞"

It was just an ordinary summer day… Until two little bundles of fluff turned it into something magical. Masi's rabbits hopped into our lives with tiny paws, twitchy noses, and more love than we ever imagined. From that day on, every cuddle became a memory, every hop a heartbeat, and every moment — a story we'll tell forever. Welcome, little ones. You didn't just fill our home… You filled our hearts. 💖🐇✨

2024

Magical Memories in Bikaner

"A Royal Desert Retreat That Captured Our Hearts"

In 2022, we escaped to Bikaner, a gem of Rajasthan's desert. Amid grand havelis and golden dunes, we created memories wandering ancient palaces, savoring Rajasthani cuisine, and watching sunsets light up the sky. A camel safari under the vast stars reminded us that adventure still binds our hearts. This trip was more than a getaway—it was a celebration of our enduring love.

2024

Mehr House

"After much struggle, a dream fulfilled..."

Years of dreams wrapped in bricks and cement... Countless nights of silent prayers, endless calculations, and held-back tears. Every corner of this house echoes with our journey — from laughter around a single fan to finally switching on the lights of Mehr. It wasn't just a house we built... It was hope, stitched together with sacrifices. It was love, tested by time and held strong by belief. And now, when we step in — we don’t just see walls… we see us. Our chaos, our comfort. Our struggle, our sanctuary. Our dream — fulfilled.

2025

Our Dream Wheels

"The next chapter of our journey — Mahindra BE 6"

Every family story has a chapter about the perfect car — and ours is about to begin with the stunning Mahindra BE 6. More than just a vehicle, it represents our family's next adventure together. Soon, we'll create countless memories on new roads, with new horizons, but the same love that has carried us through 21 beautiful years.

A beautiful family isn't just about being related. It's about being united in heart and soul, supporting each other through every season of life, and creating a legacy of love that will last for generations to come. Together, we are stronger, brighter, and infinitely blessed.

2025

Twenty-one years of laughter and tears. Twenty-one years of holding hands through life's storms. Twenty-one years of growing together, dreaming together, and loving each other more with each passing day. As they stand in their dream home today, they look back at their journey with gratitude and forward to their future with hope. This is not just a celebration of the past, but a beautiful promise for all the adventures yet to come.
